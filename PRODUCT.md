# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Casual players sharing one keyboard or a touch screen for a fast local two-player race.

## Product Purpose

Provide an immediately playable two-player racing game with a realistic roadside scene, obstacles, manual shifting, and clear win conditions.

## Brand Personality

Energetic, competitive, arcade-like.

## Anti-references

Avoid menu-heavy flows, abstract test tracks, single-player-only controls, and decorative screens that delay play.

## Design Principles

Start on the race surface.
Keep both players equally visible.
Make speed and gear changes readable at a glance.
Use obstacles as active gameplay, not background decoration.
Keep restarts instant.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use high-contrast HUD text, keyboard and touch controls, and avoid relying on color alone for player status.
